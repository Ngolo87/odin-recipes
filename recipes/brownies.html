Brownies

Ingredients

Brownie batter

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder

Frosting

  • 3 TBSP butter
  • 3 TBSP unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 TBSP honey
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup confectioners' sugar

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour 8-inch square pan
  2. Melt 1.2 cup butter in large saucepan. Remove from heat, and stir in surgar eggs and 2 tsp vanilla extract. Beat in 1/3 cup cocoa, flour, salt and baking powder. Spread batter into pan
  3. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until top is dry and edges start to pull away from side of pan. Let cool
  4. To make frostint combine softened butter, confectioners' sugar, 3 TBSP cocoa, honey and vanilla extract in bowl and stir until smooth. Frost brownies while still warm.
