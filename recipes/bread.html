No-Knead Bread Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 1/4 cups (425 g) all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1/2 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water (100-110 degrees F)

Instructions

  1. Mix dough - Stir together flour, salt and yeast in large bowl. Add warm water and stir with wooden spoon or spatula until dough is evenly mixed with no large flour streaks. Dough should be loose.
  2. Let dough rise - Cover bowl with plastic wrap and let dough rest at room temperature for 12-18 hours
  3. Shape dough - Transfer dough to well floured surface. Fold the dough on top of itself pulling the outer edges up and into the center a few times until dough feels tighter and holds shape in round dough ball. Lightly flour, turn upside down and place on parchment paper. Cover with plastic wrap while oven heats
  4. Pre-heat oven and heat Dutch oven - Pre-heat oven to 450 degrees. Place dutch oven in oven for 20-30 minutes while dough rests
  5. Bake - Carefully using oven mits remove dutch oven from oven. Lift edges of parchment paper and place dough ball (with parchment paper) into Dutch oven. Bake for 30 minutes. Remove lid and bake for another 20-30 minutes until crust reached desired level of crisp
  6. Serve - Remove from oven, and carefully lift edges of parchment paper to transfer the bread to a wire cooling rack. Let cool for 30 minutes then enjoy!
