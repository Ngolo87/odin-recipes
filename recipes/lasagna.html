Lasagna

Ingredients

Meat sauce

  • 2 tbsp extra virign olive oil
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 3/4 cup onion, diced
  • 3/4 cup bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 28 oz (1 can) tomato sauce
  • 3 oz (1/2 can) tomato paste
  • 14 oz (1 can) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh or dried oregano
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley packed
  • 1 tbsp Italien seasoning
  • 1 pinch of garlic powder or garlic salt
  • 1 tbsp of red or white wine vinegar
  • Salt to taste

Other Ingredients

  • 9 dry lasagna noodles
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 24 oz mozzarella cheese grated or sliced
  • 4 oz freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Put pasta water to boil - Put large pot of salted water to boil. While waiting for water to boil start preparing meat sauce
  2. Brown ground beef- In large skillet heat 2 tsp of olive oil on medium high heat. Add ground beek and cook until lightly brown on all sides. Remove beef with slotted spoon to a bowl. Drain off all but a tbsp of fat
  3. Cook bell pepper, onions and garlic and add back beef - Add diced bell pepper and onions to skillet. Cook for 4-5 minutes until onion are translucent and peppers are softened. Add minced garlic and cook for 30 seconds until fragrant. Return browned ground beef to pan and stir to combine. Reduce heat to low and cook for 5 minutes
  4. Combine sauce - Transfer beef mixture to medium sized pot. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce and tomatoes paste to pot. Add spices - oregano, italien seasoning and garlic salt. Add wine vinegar and salt. Bring sauce to simmer then lower heat to maintain low simmer. Cook for 15-45 minutes stirring often. Scrape bottom of pot to ensure nothing sticks to the bottom. Remove from heat
  5. Cook Noodles- Once water from step 1 is boiling add dry lasagna noddles and cook according to package until al dente. Stir often to prevent sticking. When ready drain in colander and rinse with cold water. Coat noodles in olive oil and lay on baking sheet to prevent sitcking together
  6. Preheat oven to 375 F
  7. Assemble lasagna - In 9x13 inch casserole, ladle enough sauce too cover boottom of dish. Arrange one layer of lasagna nodles over the sauce. Cover noodles with a layer of sauce. Add layer of grated mozzarella cheese, dolops of ricotta cheese evenly and sprinkle half parmesan cheese. Apply second layer of noodles and repeat meat and cheese layers as previously described. Finish with layer of noodles covered in cheese.
  8. Bake - cover with aluminum foil tented to not touch cheese. Bake at 375 for 45 minutes. Uncover for last 10 minutes to allow cheese to brown.
  9. Cool and Serve- Remove from oven and allow to cool for 15 minutes before serving.
